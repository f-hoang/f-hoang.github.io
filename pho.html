Vietnamese Beef Pho

Hey baby, this is a recipe webpage for vietnamese beef pho that you can make in a pressure cooker which would take about 1 hour prep and 1 hour cook.

Ingredients

  • 3lbs Beef bones
  • Onions
  • Rock salt
  • Fish sauce
  • Ginger

Instructions

  1. First blanch the bones to remove scum and impurities
  2. Char the onions and ginger until fragrant
  3. Place bones, onions, and ginger into the pressure cooker.
  4. Add water, rock salt, fish sauce, and peppercorns to the pot.
